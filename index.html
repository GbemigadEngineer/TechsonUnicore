🎉 Company Milestone March 2025

Techson Unicore is Officially CAC Registered

After months of building, shipping, and believing in the vision, Techson Unicore has taken a defining step: we are now officially registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria.

This is not just a legal formality. For us, CAC registration represents the formal beginning of something we have been working toward since the idea of Techson Unicore was first conceived — a credible, accountable, and ambitious technology company built to serve African businesses at every level.

From our first client website to the four digital products currently in development, every milestone has been a proof of concept. This registration is the institutional backing that makes those milestones mean even more.

"We didn't register to say we exist on paper. We registered because we intend to build something that lasts — a company that Nigerian businesses can trust, grow with, and call a genuine partner in their digital journeys."

— Oduwole Oluwagbemiga Samuel, Founder & CEO, Techson Unicore

The registration also opens new doors: formal partnerships, institutional clients, government contracts, and the ability to operate at a scale that unregistered ventures cannot. It is, in every sense, a foundation.

But more than the business opportunities it unlocks, the registration is a statement of intent. Techson Unicore is here to stay.

"Every great company started somewhere. We chose to start with integrity — with the right structures in place from day one. That's the kind of company we are building."

— Oduwole Oluwagbemiga Samuel, Founder & CEO, Techson Unicore

As we celebrate this milestone, we remain focused on what comes next: delivering exceptional client work, shipping our first product, and continuing to build the team and culture that will define Techson Unicore for years to come.

Thank you to our clients, supporters, and everyone who believed in this vision from the beginning. The best is very much still ahead.
